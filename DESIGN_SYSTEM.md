# EnglishLegalese.com v1.54.3 Design System

## Rule
Visual polish only. No feature expansion. No destructive changes. No removed functionality.

## Brand
Primary public brand: EnglishLegalese.com

## Visual direction
Premium legal education + modern AI academy.

## Palette
- Navy: professional trust
- Teal: learning/action accent
- Gold: premium highlight only
- Warm ivory/white: clean education background

## Layout
- Reduce excessive vertical gaps.
- Keep section rhythm consistent.
- Prioritize one primary CTA.
- Keep public pages separate from internal operations.

## Typography
- Elegant serif display headings.
- Readable professional body text.
- Avoid oversized headlines that wrap awkwardly.

## Components
- Consistent cards
- Consistent buttons
- Consistent icons
- Consistent navigation
